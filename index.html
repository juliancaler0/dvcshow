Welcome to the Diablo Valley Card Show

The East Bay's most exciting destination for collectors, hobbyists, and fans. Whether you're hunting for a PSA 10 rookie, chasing vintage Pokemon pulls, or looking to flip your collection — this is the spot.

We bring together over 120 vendors under one roof with everything from graded sports cards and sealed wax to vintage toys, comics, and more. Come shop, trade, and connect with the collector community.
