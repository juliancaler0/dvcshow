How to Edit

Click any text on the page to edit it directly. Hover over any image and click "Change Image" to upload a replacement. Use Quick Links to update URLs everywhere at once. Click Save & Publish when done.

How the Editor Works

  1. Click any text on the page in the preview area — headings, paragraphs, dates — and just start typing.
  2. Hover over an image (flyer, logo, past event photos) and click the green "Change Image" button to upload a new one. PNG, JPG, WEBP, GIF accepted.
  3. Use the sidebar to update the Ticket URL and Instagram URL. These get applied to every matching link on the site.
  4. To add a new Past Event card, click the button in the sidebar. To remove one, hover over a card and click its delete button.
  5. When you're happy, click Save & Publish. The previous version is automatically backed up — restore from the sidebar if needed.
  6. If something goes wrong, just refresh this page to discard all unsaved edits.
